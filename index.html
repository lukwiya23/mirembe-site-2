THE PROBLEM

Poverty has had a major impact across Uganda, especially in the rural areas where the daily lives of single struggling mothers and their children are greatly affected.

OUR SOLUTION

To provide a platform for mothers to learn different skills to tackle poverty and access means to provide for their children. Through education and material support, the welfare of single mothers and their children in rural areas will be refined as an initial step towards financial freedom.
